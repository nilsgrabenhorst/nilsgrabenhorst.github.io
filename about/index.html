About

Hello!

I am Nils Grabenhorst, a software engineer focusing on the Swift language. Originally trained as an architect (buildings, not software), I got a little taste of the joy of programming when dipping my toes into developing parametric components for the CAD/BIM package we used. This inspired me to go back to school and study Computer Science. I loved it, especially the classes Software Engineering, Advanced Algorithms, and Computational Geometry.

Since graduating in 2012, I have been working for Accenture Song Design, developing native iOS apps. In early 2025 I decided to go indy. I am very excited to find out where this journey will take me!

Nice to meet you!
